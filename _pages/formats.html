Tiny Leaders

Website: http://tinyleaders.blogspot.com/

The original format created by Bramwell Tackaberry and Steven Hamonic, while it's lovingly thought of it has not been updated since 2nd of August 2021, and it's therefore not recommended to use.

Tiny Leaders: Reborn

Website: https://tiny-leaders-reborn.blogspot.com/
Dedicated Discord server

Created by a group of fans to the original Tiny Leaders in 2017 after the original Creators had all but abandoned the Tiny Leaders format

Pickled Tiny Leaders

Website: https://sites.google.com/view/tlcp

Created as a middle step of Tiny Leaders, it's goal is to preserve a common ground to build upon. It is more similar to the original format than Tiny Leaders: Reborn
