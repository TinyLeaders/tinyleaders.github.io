Tiny Leaders

Tiny Leaders is a singleton Magic: the Gathering format with tiny cost cards, in a tiny sized deck.


All cards in your deck must have a mana value of three or less, you play with exactly 50 cards decks, and you are also allowed to have a sideboard of exactly 10 cards. Your deck will also have to follow your commander color identity.

While the deck shares some similarities with Commander it is recommended as a 1v1 format.

There are multiple sub formats of Tiny Leaders (that you can read about here), of these one Reborn is the biggest and will be the only one this website highlights. The ban list, rules and faq are all for the Reborn format.

A Magic: the Gathering® format consisting of tiny cards in a tiny deck.
