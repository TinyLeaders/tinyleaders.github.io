Tiny Leaders

Banned List

The Tiny Leaders cardpool consists of all regulation-sized Magic cards publicly released by Wizards of the Coast other than those with silver borders, gold borders or acorn-shaped security stamps.

Banned List

  • All cards that refer to the ante mechanic (full list here)
  • All cards that Wizards has removed from constructed formats (full list here)
  • All cards with the Conspiracy card type (full list here)
